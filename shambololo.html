About Shambololo

Shambololo is a quiet camping area beside a dam, best for guests who bring their own tent and camping gear. The site is aimed at self-sufficient campers who want firepit evenings, fishing and nature watching rather than built accommodation.

Scenic Dam View

Shaded Lapa Area

Braai and Relaxation Spot

Imagine waking up to the serene view of a dam stretching out before you, surrounded by lush green grass and shaded by trees. This is Shambololo's main campsite — a peaceful haven where you can relax under the lapa, enjoy a barbecue at the braai area, and spend lazy afternoons in the shade. It's the perfect spot for happy camping, with plenty of space to unwind and connect with nature.

Facilities

Lapa (Shade)
Rest comfortably under the tin-roofed lapa during the day. Tables and benches are provided.
Water Point
Fresh water available on site for drinking (at your own risk), washing, and cooking.
Shower & Toilet Block
Clean shared ablution facilities with flush toilets and hot showers.
Hot Shower
Hot water available from the donkey boiler system. Three showers available. If the boiler is not running, cold water showers are available. Ask the manager to start it.
Flush Toilet
Well-maintained shared toilet facilities on site. Three toilets available.
Washbasin
Convenient hand-washing stations near ablution areas.
Braai Area
Space for braai/barbecue with braai stands. Firewood available locally.
Firepit
Communal firepit perfect for evening gatherings and stargazing. Firewood can be used. Ignition service available.
Waste Bin
Rubbish disposal areas to keep the campsite clean.
Fenced & Gated
Secure perimeter with fenced and gated entrance at the facility gate. (Note: The campsite is not immediately next to the gate.)

Not Available

  • No huts, lodges, chalets or built sleeping rooms — this is tent camping only.
  • No cooking utensils, pots, pans or plates are provided on site.
  • No shop, restaurant or on-site food service is available.

Getting There

Google Maps location available Search for "Shambololo Campsite" on Google Maps or use the link below. Confirm exact arrival instructions with Clever before you travel.

Open Shambololo Campsite in Google Maps

From Lusaka: Take the Great East Road (T1) south towards Chirundu. After approximately 40-45 minutes (about 50km from Lusaka city center), look for the turn-off to Shambololo on the left side of the road. The exact location is marked on Google Maps above. Always contact Clever in advance for the latest directions and to confirm the gate will be open.

How to Book

1

Message Clever on WhatsApp

Send your dates and number of campers to +260 979 337 931. He'll confirm availability and give you exact directions to the lodge.

2

Agree on arrival time

The gate is kept locked. Clever needs to know when to expect you — agree on a window so he can meet you at the entrance.

3

Pay on arrival

150 ZMW per person per night, paid on the day. Bring cash — there is no card machine on site.
