Camper Tips

1

WhatsApp Clever the day before to confirm your spot — the gate is locked and he needs to know you're coming. Don't just show up.

2

Bring everything you need. The closest shop is back towards Lusaka, roughly 25–30 minutes away. There is nothing to buy at the lodge.

3

The donkey boiler takes about 45 minutes to heat up. Tell Clever when you plan to shower so he can start it in time.

4

Fishing is best in the early morning. Bring your own rod and a valid Zambia Fisheries permit if you plan to keep your catch.

5

Mosquitoes are active at dusk — long sleeves, DEET, and a net for your tent make the evenings much more pleasant.

— Tips from a repeat visitor, March 2025

How to Book

1

Message Clever on WhatsApp

Send your dates and number of campers to +260 979 337 931. He'll confirm availability and give you exact directions to the lodge.

2

Agree on arrival time

The gate is kept locked. Clever needs to know when to expect you — agree on a window so he can meet you at the entrance.

3

Pay on arrival

150 ZMW per person per night, paid on the day. Bring cash — there is no card machine on site.
